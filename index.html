About

Why this template?

It’s a great starting point for portfolios, product pages, hackathon demos, or internal tools.

  • Sticky translucent navbar
  • Smooth scrolling

Quick tips

  • Replace <title> and the hero text.
  • Swap brand colors in :root.
  • Use .grid, .card, and .row to layout content.

FAQ

Is this really just one file?

Yep. No npm, no bundlers—just open in a browser or ship to static hosting.

How do I change colors?

Edit the CSS variables in :root at the top of the file.

Can I add sections?

Copy a .section block and tweak the content. The layout just works.
